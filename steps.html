Steps

Add the convenience of a durable and attractive spa step to your Cal Spas hot tub environment. Access your spa with ease to enjoy a warm soak in your hot tub. Spa steps also second as a storage component to help keep towels and slippers off the ground. Available in a variety of sizes, colors and styles, Cal Spas has a spa step to match your outdoor home décor.

Whether you are shopping for an elegant spa step, or prefer a smaller yet functional spa step, Cal Spas has got you covered! Each spa step manufactured by Cal Spas is designed to deliver style while offering a convenient alternative to accessing your spa. Supplement any spa step and further your accessibility with optional handrails also available by Cal Spas.
